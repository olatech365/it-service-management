What is IT Service Management?

IT Service Management (ITSM) refers to the processes and strategies used by organizations to design, deliver, manage, and improve IT services. It focuses on aligning IT services with the needs of the business.

Instead of just managing hardware or software, ITSM focuses on delivering value through structured IT service processes.

Common ITSM processes include:

Incident management

Problem management

Change management

Service request management

IT asset management

These processes help businesses ensure reliable and consistent IT service delivery.

Importance of IT Service Management

Implementing effective IT Service Management practices provides several advantages for businesses.

Improved Service Efficiency

ITSM frameworks help standardize processes, reducing downtime and improving service delivery.

Better IT Support

Structured IT service processes ensure faster issue resolution and improved customer satisfaction.

Cost Optimization

Efficient IT resource management helps organizations reduce operational costs.

Improved Security and Compliance

Proper IT management ensures compliance with industry regulations and security standards.

Key Components of IT Service Management

Service desk management

These components help organizations maintain stable and efficient IT operations.
